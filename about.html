Our Story

Started small.
Built with purpose.

Great Gaining Institution was founded in 2022 by a group of IT professionals who saw a clear gap — engineering graduates with degrees but no practical skills, struggling to crack even entry-level interviews.

We started with a single batch of 20 students in a small classroom in Chennai. Today, we have trained over 2,000 students across 7 courses, with a 95% placement rate that speaks for itself.

Our approach has always been simple: teach what companies actually need, not what textbooks say. Every course is built around real job descriptions, real tools, and real projects.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience to join?
No. Most of our courses are designed for absolute beginners. We start from the basics and build up gradually. A basic understanding of computers is enough.
What is the batch schedule? Are there weekend batches?
We offer both weekday (Mon–Fri, 2 hrs/day) and weekend (Sat–Sun, 4 hrs/day) batches. New batches start every month. You can choose what fits your schedule.
Is placement guaranteed after the course?
We provide full placement assistance — resume building, mock interviews, and direct referrals to our 50+ hiring partners. While we cannot legally guarantee a job, 95% of our students get placed within 3 months of completing the course.
What certificate will I receive?
You will receive a course completion certificate from Great Gaining Institution. This certificate is recognised by our hiring partners and can be added to your LinkedIn profile and resume.
Are there any EMI or instalment options for fees?
Yes. We offer a 2-instalment payment plan for all courses — 60% at the time of enrolment and 40% after the first month. Contact us to discuss your options.
